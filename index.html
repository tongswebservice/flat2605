Emergency situation & Contact

In case of any emergency during your stay, please refer to the following instructions:

Hot Water Issues

  • Please check the hot water from both the bathroom and kitchen taps first.
  • Sometimes, it may take a few minutes for the water to heat up.
  • If the building's master boiler is down, it may temporarily affect the hot water supply. Please contact us if the issue persists.

Power Outage / Electricity Issues

  • If the electricity goes off, please locate the master switchboard above the washing machine, on the right-hand side of the wallinside the metal case.
  • Open the metal case and check if any switches have tripped.
  • Please take a picture of the switchboard and send it to us for verification.
